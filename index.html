Your Questions Answered

Most reports are ready within 2–4 hours. Urgent reports are prioritized and can be ready in under 90 minutes. You'll be notified via WhatsApp.

For abdominal sonography, fasting for 4–6 hours is recommended. For pregnancy sonography and other scans, no preparation is needed. We'll guide you when you book.

Dr. Vasundhara personally conducts all sonography and mammography examinations. Her availability can be confirmed while booking your appointment.
